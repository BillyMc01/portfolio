Children's Reading Application

Following is a UI/UX Prototype for a reading application designed to be used by 4-8 year olds with supervison from a parent or gardiun. The application is designed to include a number of story books and quizzes appropriate for the targeted age group . The prototype draws many of its design elements from Don Norman's five design principles as well as background research into how the target groupuses mobile applications. A total of six published research papers where used to decide on the specific designs that the application would require to suit the needs of the target audience.
